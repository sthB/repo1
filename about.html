Growing up in beautiful village of southern Nepal and working in farm with his grandfather, he developed his love for outdoor in his early age. His passion for art has always focused on affection for the untrammeled country life. Bimal Spent much of his youth hiking and painting in the mountain wilderness. He gives credit to his grandfather for influencing him in art.He started painting with brushes made out of bamboo stick and pigments collected in countryside by his grandfather. A devotion in art was obvious at an early age. Bimal moved to United State in 2007 and started painting as a part time while continuing his engineering career. He is also affiliated with prestigious art organization CAC( California Art Club) and OPA(Oil Painters of America). Bimal paints at his Hayward studio and travels frequently for pleinair painting.
